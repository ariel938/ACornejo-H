Clash Royale is a real-time multiplayer strategy game developed by Supercell, combining elements of tower defense, collectible card games, and multiplayer online battle arenas. Players collect and upgrade cards featuring Clash of Clans troops, spells, and defenses, then use them to battle opponents in fast-paced matches. The goal is to destroy the opponent's towers while defending your own, with a special focus on the central King's Tower, whose destruction results in an instant win. Players must carefully manage elixir, a regenerating resource, to deploy their cards strategically during the three-minute battles. With its competitive ladder system, clan features, and regular updates, Clash Royale offers a dynamic and highly strategic gaming experience.
